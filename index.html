Welcome Aboard

About 757 Tiki Boat Tours

There is nothing quite like cruising the beautiful waters of Hampton, Virginia aboard a one-of-a-kind tiki boat. At 757 Tiki Boat Tours, we have created the ultimate floating island experience, combining tropical vibes with the scenic beauty of the Chesapeake Bay region. Whether you are celebrating a special occasion or just looking for an unforgettable day on the water, our custom-built tiki boat delivers an experience like no other.

Our 30-foot custom tiki boat is fully equipped with everything you need for the perfect cruise. From Bluetooth speakers pumping your favorite playlist to a blender ready for frozen cocktails, we have thought of every detail. Bring your own food and drinks, kick off your shoes, and let the captain navigate you through some of the most stunning waterways on the East Coast.

We pride ourselves on offering intimate, small-group experiences as well as larger bareboat charters for groups up to 12. Dogs are always welcome, and yes, there is a restroom onboard. From sunset cruises to bachelorette parties, every trip is a custom adventure tailored to you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you book your tiki boat adventure.

We offer 2, 3, and 4-hour cruises to fit your schedule and adventure level. Whether you want a quick sunset sail or a full day of island hopping, we have you covered.
Absolutely! We are 100% BYOB. Bring whatever food and beverages you like. We even have a blender onboard so you can whip up frozen cocktails, smoothies, or margaritas while you cruise.
Yes! Dogs are absolutely welcome aboard our tiki boat. Many of our guests bring their furry friends along for the ride, and they love it just as much as their humans do.
Yes, our tiki boat is equipped with a full onboard restroom for your comfort and convenience. No need to worry about long cruises without facilities.
Our custom 30ft tiki boat features Bluetooth speakers for your music, comfortable seating with shaded areas, a large cooler to keep your drinks cold, a blender for frozen cocktails, and a full onboard restroom.
Swimming is allowed at the captain's discretion based on current weather and water conditions. We love a good swim stop, and when conditions are right, it is one of the best parts of the experience.
A bareboat charter means the customer controls and operates the vessel. This option is required for groups of 7 or more passengers and can accommodate up to 12 guests. It gives your group full freedom to cruise at your own pace.
Tipping is not required but is greatly appreciated. The customary range is 15-25% of the tour price, depending on your experience. Our captains work hard to make every cruise special.
Refunds are issued if your spot is filled by another booking or if the captain cancels due to weather conditions. We always prioritize safety and will work with you to reschedule if needed.
Park near the tan building and dumpster at 374 Dandy Haven Rd, Hampton, VA 23664. You will see the dock area from the parking spot. We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes before your scheduled departure.
Small group tours with a captain accommodate up to 6 passengers. Large group bareboat charters can hold up to 12 passengers. For groups of 7 or more, you will need to book a bareboat charter where you captain the vessel yourself.
Yes! We specialize in bachelorette parties and private celebrations. Our tiki boat is the perfect venue for a unique celebration with speakers for music, a blender for party drinks, and gorgeous water views. Contact us for custom package details.
